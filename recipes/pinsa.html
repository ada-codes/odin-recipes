Pinsa Romana

Description

Pinsa with old world flavors such as fontina cheese, olive oil, pine nuts, and spinach. Ready in just two steps. Original recipe found at deliciousmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, slice thinly
  • 1 garlic clove, slice thinly
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 250g spinach leaves, washed
  • 2 pinsa romana bases
  • 125g fontina cheese or mozzarella, sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Fry, fry, fry! Heat the oil in a frying pan at low-medium heat. Throw in the garlic, onion, thyme, salt and pepper and fry for for 10 minutes until they're nice and soft. Add the spinach and cook 1-2 minutes or until wilted. Remove heat, allow to cool.
  2. Bake, bake, bake! Heat your oven to 450 Fahrenheit. Divide the spinach mix evenly between each base, then top with the cheeses and pine nuts. Cook in the oven for 6-8 minutes until golden brown.

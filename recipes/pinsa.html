Pinsa Romana

Description

Pinsa with old world flavors such as goat cheese, beetroot, pine nuts, and spinach. Ready in just two steps. Original recipe found at deliciousmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, slice thinly
  • 1 garlic clove, slice thinly
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 250g spinach leaves, washed
  • 2 pinsa romana bases
  • 125g fontina cheese or mozzarella, sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan

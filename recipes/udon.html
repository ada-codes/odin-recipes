Kitsune Udon

Description

Allegedly the most popular udon dish in Japan and named after a fox of Japanese mythology, Kitsune Udon is umami on top of umami with shiitake mushroom soaked in dashi stock and imbued with usukuchi soy sauce. Original recipe from norecipes.com

Ingredients

For udon soup

  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 4 grams konbu (a type of dried kelp)
  • 3 grams dried shiitake mushroom
  • 2 teaspoons usukuchi soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

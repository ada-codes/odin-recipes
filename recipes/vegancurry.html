Vegan Curry

Description

A creamy and savory vegan curry made with chickpeas, tomatoes, spinach, coconut milk, and warm spices. The recipe is quick and easy and can be in about 25 minutes--prep time not included. Makes 4 servings which stay tasty three days in the fridge.

Link to original recipe at acouplecooks.com

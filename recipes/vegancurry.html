Vegan Curry

Description

A creamy and savory vegan curry made with chickpeas, tomatoes, spinach, coconut milk, and warm spices. The recipe is quick and easy and can be made in about 25 minutes--prep time not included. Makes 4 servings which stay tasty three days in the fridge.

Link to original recipe at acouplecooks.com

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 medium bell peppers
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespon olive oil
  • 6 cups (5 ounces) baby spinach leaves
  • 28-ounce can crushed fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 15-ounce can chickpeas
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 3/4 cup full fat coconut milk

Recipe

  1. Prep your veggies! Dice those onions and peppers. Mince that garlic. Peel that ginger then mince it up good.
  2. Saute your veggies! Grab a large skillet and heat your oil in it over medium high heat. Toss in those onions and peppers then saute for 5 minutes. Then add the spinach, garlic, and ginger, and cook for 2 minutes or until the spinach has wilted.
  3. Sssimmer. With kindness, pour your tomatoes and their juices into the skillet. Mix in your salt and spices. Grab your chickpeas, peas, and coconut milk and add them to the skillet, too. Cook for 5 minutes until it's bubbly.
  4. Enjoy (not optional)
